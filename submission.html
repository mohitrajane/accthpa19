Submission

Papers submitted for reviewing should conform to IEEE specifications. Manuscript templates can be downloaded from IEEE website.

Paper submission is through EasyChair conference management system. For submitting paper Click Here

The maximum length of papers is 6 pages. All the papers will go through single-blind peer review process.

Short papers (accepted for poster presentation) should be up to 4 pages long. References and any other additional material must be included in the number of pages.
