ADVANCED COMPUTING AND COMMUNICATION TECHNIQUES FOR HIGH PERFORMANCE APPLICATIONS (ACCTHPA)

July 02 - 04, 2020

Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT), Cochin, Kerala, India

ABOUT

ACCTHPA 2020, the fifth International Conference on "Advanced Computing and Communication Techniques for High Performance Applications (ACCTHPA)" is a prime conferences which intends to provide a forum that brings together the researchers from academia and industry to convene and swap over the ideas and recent research work on the High Performance Applications. Scheduled on July 2-4, 2020 at Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT), Cochin, Kerala, India, ACCTHPA is the fifth edition of this conference. ACCTHPA is organized in association with IEEE Kerala Section and all accepted and presented papers will be published in IEEE Xplore.
More >

THEME

The theme for this year's conference is "Latest trends in human centered HPC applications". HPC applications has the power to realize a better future for humanity. This involves utilizing knowledge from different fields of computing. HPC applications can be developed in various fields of Science and Engineering for the progress humanity. Artificial intelligence will be the most important technology of the 21st century - augmenting knowledge from interdisciplinary areas. High Performance Computational techniques are essential for realizing this. We hope to see original contributions from interdisciplinary areas making using of HPC for the betterment of humanity.

Call for papers

ACCTHPA 2020 invites original papers that move the field of high performance computing forward, be it through the advance of theory, methodology, or practice. We invite submissions of high international standard, presenting original work that significantly contributes to research and practice of High-Performance Computing in it's broadest sense.
